Research Experience and Future Work

Since September of 2023, I have been a part-time undergraduate researcher in the Daru lab, working on computational biogeography. During Summer 2024, I was a part of the Biology Summer Undergraduate Research Program (BSURP) cohort, where I began a project examining crop wild relatives. Namely, I helped to develop an algorithm which for a particular species, finds between 10 and 20 of its closest monophyletically-clustered relatives. We are aiming for publication in Late 2025.
